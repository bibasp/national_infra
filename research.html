Beijing Normal University researchers integrated physics-informed neural networks with distributed hydrologic models to improve short-term flood forecasting accuracy across the Yangtze River basin. The hybrid approach reduced peak-flow prediction error by 38% and provided up to six hours of additional warning time for extreme events.

Groundwater Sustainability in Arid and Semi-Arid Regions

Water Resources
Institution: University of Arizona Year: 2025

The University of Arizona’s Hydrogeology Program applied GRACE satellite gravimetry and InSAR land-subsidence monitoring to quantify groundwater depletion in the US-Mexico borderlands. Findings informed state policy, leading to new managed aquifer recharge zones projected to halt 65% of annual overdraft by 2030.
